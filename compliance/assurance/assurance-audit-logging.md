## <a name="how-does-microsoft-365-centralize-and-report-on-audit-logs"></a><span data-ttu-id="8367a-120">Comment Microsoft 365 centralise-t-il et signale-t-il les journaux d’audit ?</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8367a-120">How does Microsoft 365 centralize and report on audit logs?</span></span>
