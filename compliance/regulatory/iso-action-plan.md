The ISO/IEC 27000 family of standards outlines controls and mechanisms that help maintain the security of information assets.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="58c83-p102">ISO/IEC 27001 est la norme internationale d’implémentation d’un système de gestion de sécurité des informations (ISMS).
